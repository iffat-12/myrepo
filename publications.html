Early Detection of Alzheimer's Disease Using Personalized GANs-Based Federated Learning: A Data-Driven Approach

I. Fatima, U. Iqbal, U. Ishtiaq, and B. U. I. Khan

Journal: Makara Journal of Technology
Abstract Preview: Standard federated learning models face client-side data distribution issues when training medical images across different hospitals. This research details a decentralized, GAN-based personalized framework targeting early-stage Alzheimer's disease diagnosis on MRI brain scans.
